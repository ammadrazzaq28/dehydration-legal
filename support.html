Frequently asked questions

How do I log a drink?

Open the Home tab, tap the add button, choose a drink type and amount, then confirm. Your daily progress updates immediately.

How is my daily goal calculated?

By default, DeHydration estimates a goal from your profile (weight, activity, weather). You can switch to a custom goal in Settings.

Why don't I see my steps?

Steps require Apple Health permission. Go to Settings → Health → Data Access & Devices → DeHydration and allow Step Count. If you previously denied access, enable it there.

How do I cancel DeHydration Pro?

Subscriptions are managed by Apple. On your iPhone: Settings → [your name] → Subscriptions → DeHydration → Cancel Subscription. Cancel at least 24 hours before renewal to avoid the next charge.

How do I restore my purchase?

On the subscription screen in the App, tap Restore. Make sure you are signed in with the same Apple ID used for the original purchase.

How do I reset my data?

Use the reset option in the App's Settings screen, or delete and reinstall the App. This removes locally stored hydration history on your device.

Is DeHydration medical advice?

No. The App is for general wellness tracking only. See our health disclaimer and consult a healthcare professional for medical guidance.

How do I request a refund?

Refunds are processed by Apple. Visit reportaproblem.apple.com and sign in with your Apple ID.
